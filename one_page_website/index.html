About

Some photographers spend their entire careers searching for a signature style. Brandon Woelfel—a young, and enormously popular Instagram influencer—found his style early and has become an Internet sensation as a result. Brandon notes that his whimsical images—which feature distinct soft tones and pops of brightly colored hues as well as some magical post-processing—not only capture a sense of wonder, but also reflects a balance of professional passion and a keen sense of what will attract new followers online.
