Blu Jam Cafe

$15-$20

The best way to describe the variety of titillating dishes is “Creatively Eclectic." You will not find many typical breakfast items on the menu. Instead, enjoy the Brunch Carbonara – Gluten-Free Risotto with crispy smoked bacon, green peas, parsley and parmesan, topped with two poached eggs and a herb pistou. Once you see and experience one of the signature dishes, like the Crunchy French Toast with a warm Vanilla Bean sauce, you will know why Blu Jam Cafe has been awarded the best breakfast in Los Angeles two years in a row.

  • Crunchy French Toast ($13.95)
  • French Toast Churros ($12.95)
  • Brunch Carbonara ($14.95)
  • Blu Jam Benedict ($14.95)
  • Spicy Vegan Tex Mex Tofu Hash ($13.95)
